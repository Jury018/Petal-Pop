How to Play

Welcome to Petal Pop!

  • Match 3 or more candies of the same color to score points.
  • Match 4 candies to create a striped candy.
  • Match 5 candies in a T or L shape to create a wrapped candy.
  • Match 5 candies in a straight line to create a color bomb.

Special combos give bonus points. Good luck!
